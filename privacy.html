Privacy Policy

This policy describes how the Quote Tool ("the Software"), an internal application used by Kū Dynamics LLC, processes data. The Software is not offered as a service to third parties; it is used solely for Kū Dynamics' own sales-and-invoicing workflow.

What we process

  • Client (customer) records: company name, billing and shipping addresses, business contact details (name, email, phone, title) of the customer's procurement / accounting staff.
  • Vendor records: company name, billing and shipping addresses of vendors we purchase parts from.
  • Sales documents: quotes, purchase orders, invoices, line-item details, pricing, shipping charges, and related notes.
  • Integration tokens: OAuth access and refresh tokens for QuickBooks Online, stored encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM.

Why we process it

  • To produce internal quotes, purchase orders, and invoices.
  • To synchronize invoices and customer / vendor records to our own QuickBooks Online company via the Intuit API.
  • To maintain accounting and audit records for legal and regulatory purposes.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share the data with any third party beyond:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online, where invoices and customer / vendor records are pushed via the Intuit API into our own QBO company.
  • Vendors and customers, as required to fulfill the underlying business transaction (e.g. emailing a customer their invoice or a vendor their purchase order).
  • Auditors, regulators, or counsel as required by law.

What we do not do

  • We do not perform end-user analytics, advertising tracking, or behavioral profiling.
  • We do not process consumer personal data outside of business contact information voluntarily provided in a B2B context.
  • We do not transfer integration tokens or business records to any party other than Intuit and our own infrastructure.

Security

Integration tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. The underlying database volume is encrypted at rest with BitLocker (XTS-AES 256). All network traffic is encrypted in transit using HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Daily encrypted backups are retained for 14 days. All data is stored on infrastructure controlled by Kū Dynamics. Access is restricted to authorized employees and contractors.

Data retention

Records are retained for the duration of the business relationship and for the periods required by accounting, contract, and tax retention rules. Audit logs are retained for at least one year. Backups follow a 14-day rolling retention; deleted records age out of backups within that window.

Security incidents

If we discover that data covered by this policy has been improperly accessed or disclosed, we will notify Intuit and affected parties within 72 hours of discovery.

Your choices

Customers and vendors who would like to review or correct the information we hold about them may contact us at support@ku-dynamics.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the Software evolves. Changes will be posted to this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
